平靜的感覺像是水面，映照著蘇格蘭北部群島的一個夏季夜晚，即將入夜的天幕既粉且藍 ，那是如同夢一般的景色，存在於存在與不存在之間，時間是莫比烏斯環，周而復始的前 進著。
A sense of calm, like the surface of water,
reflecting a summer night in the northern Scottish isles.
The sky, on the verge of dusk, is both pink and blue         —
a dreamlike landscape suspended between existence and nonexistence.
Time moves like a Möbius strip, advancing in an endless loop.
